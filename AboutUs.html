About Me


My Name is Peter Freymueller. I am 19 years old from Fairbanks, Alaska. I created the website as

a class project. When you arrive at the website your first impression might have been that I did


not take this seriously, however you are mistaken. There is an increasing market for ouright useless impulse


purchases. If you do not believe me, watch this video: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Z3uXGGgOvR0


Because there is such a large market for pointless purchases, I would take advantage of that.


I theorize that people will see the website as a joke, and word will spread about the site because


of it. People will buy the items impulsively because it is funny, as well as they will have an


internal pull towards my products to see if they are the real deal. The funny contradictory items


will draw attention towards the site and the soap will work as an add on. Soap is a commodity.


I am able to mark up the prices on my soap higher than a general store because it comes from my


brand. People will tell their friends they bought battery powered battery chargers off my website


along with soap that they paid an extra dollar for. The soap will serve as an add on, for instance


someone may buy something because it is funny, but at the same time they see the soap and think to


themselves, "Well I do need soap around the house, might as well get some and bundle it in the same


order." This is where my profit comes from. This is how I get people to buy my product.
